## Equipment Setup & Placement

### Optimal Router Placement

For best performance:

- **Central location** in your home or office
- **Elevated position** (shelf or wall-mounted)
- **Open space** away from walls and obstructions
- **Avoid interference** from microwaves, cordless phones, thick walls
- **Near your most-used devices** for wired connections

### Mesh Extender Placement

If your plan includes mesh extenders:

- Place **halfway** between router and areas needing coverage
- Ensure extender can "see" the main router (avoid thick walls)
- Use the **LED indicators** to confirm good signal strength
- **Hardwire via Ethernet** for maximum performance (optional)
