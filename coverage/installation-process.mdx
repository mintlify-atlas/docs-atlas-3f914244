## What Happens During Installation

### Fiber Optic Line Installation

Our technician will:

- Run fiber optic cable from the street connection point to your building
- Install an Optical Network Terminal (ONT) at your location
- Ensure minimal visual impact and secure cable routing
- Test signal strength and connection quality

### Router & Equipment Setup

Depending on your plan, you'll receive:

- **WiFi 6 Router** (Básico and Hogar+ plans)
- **WiFi 6E Router** (Pro Gaming plan)
- **Mesh Extenders** (1 with Hogar+, 2 with Pro Gaming)

The technician will:

- Position the router in an optimal location for coverage
- Configure WiFi network name and password
- Set up mesh extenders (if included) for whole-home coverage
- Connect your devices and verify internet access

### Testing & Handoff

Before completing the installation:

- Speed tests confirm you're receiving your plan's full bandwidth
- You'll receive information about your account and support channels
- The technician will answer any questions about using your service

## What You Need to Prepare

### Before Installation Day

- Ensure someone 18+ will be present during the installation window
- Clear the area where you want the router installed
- Have your device(s) ready to connect and test
- Decide on your preferred WiFi network name

### Installation Requirements

- Access to the exterior of your building (for fiber line routing)
- A power outlet near the desired router location
- Clear pathway from entry point to installation location

## Installation FAQs

**How long does installation take?**  
Typical installations are completed in 2-4 hours, depending on your building's configuration.

**Do I need to provide any equipment?**  
No. All necessary equipment (router, cables, ONT, mesh extenders) is included with your plan.

**Can I reschedule my installation?**  
Yes. Contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment to reschedule.

**What if there are issues during installation?**  
Our technicians are trained to handle most scenarios. If installation can't be completed, we'll explain why and schedule a follow-up at no cost.

**Will installation damage my walls?**  
We use non-invasive installation methods whenever possible. Any necessary drilling or mounting is done professionally and cleanly.
