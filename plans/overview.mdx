## What's Included in All Plans

Every Telenet plan includes:

- **Pure fiber optic connection** - No shared bandwidth, no slowdowns
- **Symmetric speeds** - Equal upload and download speeds (↑↓)
- **24/7 technical support** - Always here when you need us
- **Free installation** - Professional setup at no extra cost
- **Modern WiFi routers** - WiFi 6 or WiFi 6E included
