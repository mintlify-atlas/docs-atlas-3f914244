## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?">
    Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Contact our support team to make changes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are the speeds really symmetric?">
    Absolutely. All Telenet plans feature true symmetric fiber optic connections - your upload speed matches your download speed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a contract or commitment?">
    No long-term contracts required. Service is month-to-month with flexible terms.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between WiFi 6 and WiFi 6E?">
    WiFi 6E adds support for the new 6GHz wireless band, providing faster speeds, lower latency, and less interference. It's the latest WiFi technology, included only in the Pro Gaming plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I add more Mesh extenders?">
    Yes! Additional Mesh extenders are available for purchase if you need extended coverage beyond what's included in your plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What can I do with a static IP?">
    Static IPs are great for hosting servers, remote access to your home network, running VPNs, accessing security cameras remotely, and more. Only included in Pro Gaming plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
