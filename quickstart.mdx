## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is fiber optic and what advantages does it have?">
    Fiber optic transmits data through light instead of electrical signals, allowing for much higher speeds, lower latency, and greater stability than traditional ADSL or coaxial cable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a data limit or download quota?">
    No. All our plans include unlimited data. You can browse, stream 4K video, and work remotely without worrying about limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans after signing up?">
    Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time without penalty. The change applies from the next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum contract period?">
    No minimum commitment. You can cancel the service whenever you want without early termination fees.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included with the router?">
    Yes, all plans include a latest-generation router. The Hogar+ plan includes WiFi 6, and the Pro Gaming plan includes WiFi 6E with extended coverage and mesh repeaters.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
