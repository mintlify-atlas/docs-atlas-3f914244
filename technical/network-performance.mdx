Telenet's fiber optic network delivers consistent, reliable, high-performance internet connectivity across all three speed tiers. This page provides detailed performance specifications and what you can expect from each plan.

Telenet offers three symmetric fiber optic internet plans:

|------|---------------|--------------|----------|---------------|
| **Básico** | 300 Mbps | 300 Mbps | ✓ Symmetric | $19.99 |

</Info>

## Latency and Response Time

### What is Latency?

Latency (also called "ping") is the time it takes for data to travel from your device to a server and back. It's measured in milliseconds (ms). Lower latency means faster response times.
